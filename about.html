About

A calculator built to end the guesswork.

Welcome to Semester Grade Calculator — a simple, free tool built to help students take the guesswork out of tracking their academic performance.

Our mission

We know how stressful it can be to figure out where you stand in a class, especially when your final grade depends on multiple categories like homework, quizzes, tests, and exams, each weighted differently. Our goal is to give students a fast, accurate, and easy-to-use tool that removes the confusion and lets you focus on what matters most: studying and improving.

What we offer

  • Calculate your current weighted grade based on custom categories
  • See what score you need on upcoming assignments or exams to hit your target grade
  • Drop your lowest scores automatically, if your course allows it
  • Understand standard grading scales and how they apply to your results

Why we built this

We understand that calculating grades can often be confusing and time-consuming, especially when different schools, colleges, and universities use different grading and weighting systems. That's why we built this calculator to be simple, accurate, and easy to use. It also respects your privacy — we never store the grades or academic information you enter. Whether you're using a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you can quickly calculate and track your academic progress anytime, anywhere.

Our commitment

We're committed to keeping this tool free, ad-light, and genuinely useful. We regularly update the calculator based on user feedback to make it more accurate and easier to use.

Have suggestions or found an issue? We'd love to hear from you — visit our Contact page to get in touch.
