Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have a question, found a bug, have a suggestion for a new feature, or just want to share feedback, feel free to reach out.

What you can contact us about

  • Bug reports — found something that isn't working correctly? Let us know the details (device, browser, and what happened) so we can fix it quickly.
  • Feature requests — have an idea to make the calculator more useful? We're always looking to improve.
  • General feedback — tell us what you like or what could be better.
  • Business inquiries — for partnerships or collaboration opportunities.

Before you reach out

If you're having trouble using the calculator, check that:

  • You've entered all category weights correctly (they should add up to 100%)
  • Your browser is up to date
  • JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings

Thank you for using the Semester Grade Calculator. Your feedback helps us make this tool better for every student who uses it.
