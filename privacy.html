Introduction

Semester Grade Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information while you use our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you access our calculators and other resources.

Information we collect

Our grade calculator tools run entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the grades, marks, GPA, CGPA, percentages, or any other academic information you enter into our calculators. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

We may automatically collect limited, non-personal information through analytics tools, such as your browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, and general website usage statistics to help improve our website.

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our website and to improve the user experience. Specifically, we use:

  • Google Analytics — helps us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources. This information is used only to analyze website performance and improve our content and services.

Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for additional information or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Data security

Although we do not collect personal or academic data entered into our calculators, we take reasonable measures to protect our website. All communications between your browser and our website are secured using HTTPS encryption.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
